Sail-N

We are a casual style eat out take-out restaurant with quality seafood.

Located on the shores of lake Bde Maka Ska on the west side of the Uptown area of Minneapolis at the corner of Lake Street and Calhoun Parkway.

On one side of us is the canoe and sailboard rental and on the other side is the sailing school.

Stop in for lunch or dinner after a run/walk around the lake or after a day of boating, paddling or racing on the lake.
